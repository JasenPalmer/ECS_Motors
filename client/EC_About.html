In And Out Done

The Grass Is Greener On Your Side

The name D&S Lawns is derived from the initial letters of me and my wife’s names, Deborah and Sitoi. We decided on investing our lives in providing superior lawn and property maintenance services to our communities, in the hope that we may be able to build a future for our children; to inherit the family business and our love for luscious lawns and fresh, radiant gardens. The proverb, “the grass is greener on the other side” is often used by many to describe the beauty of another person’s life, simply ignoring the uniqueness of their own. Our intention, written as our slogan, aims to remind people that the grass will always be greener on YOUR side, if you rely on us.
